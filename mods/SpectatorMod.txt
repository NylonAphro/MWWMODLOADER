Spectator Mod V 0.1
Adds new spectator abilities

spectator mode 'm', free flight 'n', lock free flight height 'b', enable/disable mouse weight 'v'
controls: pan forward 'w', pan left 'a', pan down/backwards 's', pan right 'd'
free flight mode: pan up 'q', pan down 'e'
free flight locked height mode: spin up/down 'scroll wheel'
free flight locked height mode: speed up 'shift', slow down 'space'
increase camera pitch 't', decrease camera pitch 'y', zoom in/out 'mouse scroll'
increase FOV 'x', decrease FOV 'z'
show/hide camera info 'p', reset camera zoom/pitch 'c'
increase smoothing '0', decrease smoothing '9'
