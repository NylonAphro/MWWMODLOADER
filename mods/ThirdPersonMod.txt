Third Person Mod V 0.2

INGAME:
Move mouse to rotate camera - player will face the direction the camera is looking
The player will continue to move towards the target position allowing you to move around more easily
Use the halt button (default 'space') to  stop moving 
Hold left alt to free rotate the camera
Scroll in/out to zoom
Left mouse button to move forward
Shift will move player backwards
Magicks are buggy, and will only target a short distance in front of yourself, revive will target in front of your wizard

OBSERVER:
W A S D to move camera
Shift to boost
Scroll to zoom in/out
